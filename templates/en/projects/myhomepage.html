My Very Own Homepage

Written by Amy Nguyen

Handouts: HTML Reference

We're ready to take what we learned about HTML and make our own homepages! This is your time to express yourself and share your interests with the rest of the class.

Open a new file in Notepad++ and save it as yourfullname.html. For example, I would name my file amynguyen.html. In this file, you will create your own page following this format:

<html>
  <body>
    <!-- Write about yourself here! -->
    <p>Merhaba!</p>
  </body>
</html>

You can write as much as you want! When you're done, show a section leader your work!

Ideas

You can put anything on your homepage! But here are some ideas in case you get stuck:

  • Tell us about your favorite books, movies, TV shows, music, etc.
  • Share some pictures from a recent event.
  • Talk about what you want to do in the future.
  • What do you like about computer science?

Displaying special characters

Go to this website and paste your paragraph into the text box. Click "escape" and paste that string into your HTML file instead.
